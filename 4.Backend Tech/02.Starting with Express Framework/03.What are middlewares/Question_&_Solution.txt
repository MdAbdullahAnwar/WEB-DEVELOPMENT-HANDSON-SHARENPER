Let’s go!



Ques1. What is the primary purpose of middleware in Express?

Ans: To process incomming requests and responses



Ques2. What are the three key arguments middleware functions in Express can access?

Ans: request, response, next



Ques3. What happens if a middleware does not call the next() function?

Ans: The request will be stuck and not proceed further



Ques4. In the library analogy, what is the role of the librarian at the "Security Check" desk?

Ans: To validate the user's credentials and pass them to the next desk



Ques5. In the library analogy, what does the "Book Checkout" desk represent in Express middleware?

Ans: A middleware that ends the request-response cycle
