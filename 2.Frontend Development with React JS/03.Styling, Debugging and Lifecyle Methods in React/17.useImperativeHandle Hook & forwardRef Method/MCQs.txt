1. When should you use "useImperativeHandle" hook in React?

Ans. When you want to expose specific methods of a child component to its parent



2. What is the second argument to useImperativeHandle used for?

Ans. Callback Function



3. What is the purpose of the forwardRef method in React?

Ans. To forward refs from parent to child components



4. How do you use forwardRef in a functional component?

Ans. forwardRef((props.ref)=>{...})



5. When combining useImperativeHandle and forwardRef, what are you likely trying to achieve?

Ans. Exposing specific methods of a child component to its parent using a forwarded ref
