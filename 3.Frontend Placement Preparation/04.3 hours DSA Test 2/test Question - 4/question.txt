A number is said to be sharpenerian number if the difference between any neighboring digits of number is 1 .

You have given a number X , you have to find all the X sharpenerian numbers.

Note : Take difference as absolute difference and first sharpnerian number is 10.



Example 123 is also sharpnerian number.


Example 1:

Input:

X = 2

Output: 10 12

Explanation: Diff between 1 and 0 and Diff between 1 and 2 is one.



Example 2:

Input:

X = 5

Output: 10 12 21 23 32
